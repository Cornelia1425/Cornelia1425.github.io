I'm Yiran Shu.

I have recently graduated from The Boston Architectural College, and am currently adapting to my working life in Boston. I have lived in Yellow Mountain, where I spent my childhood; Newcastle and Chengdu, where I expanded my education; Shanghai and Guangzhou, where I tasted a little work-life; Boston, where I am pursuing my dream.

I love dancing, playing the violin, and contemplating and imagining how places can be used by people ingeniously.

I look forward to meeting you.
